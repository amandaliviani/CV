Projects

One Click Secure

One Click Secure (OCC) is a device my team invented to help people who are in danger and have no time to call for help. With the help of Arduino UNO, GSM Module, GPS module, and a push button, this device will send a danger alert along with the user's location to their loved ones and the police whenever the user click the push button.

December 2017

Air Detection for Health Safety

Air Detection for Health Safety (ADHS) is a device my team made to determine the health level of the surrounding air. This device uses Arduino UNO, MQ-7 Gas Sensor, Breadboard, LED, Buzzer, and a powerbank. It classifies air health into three levels which will be displayed on the LED's color, Red-Purple-Green respectively from most polluted to the healthiest.

April 2018

Design and Implementation of Bandpass Filter on Guitar Instrument

Me and my colleagues design a scheme of bandpass filters to separate the sound of each string of the guitar. The filter input is a recording of a guitar sound played in any note and the output of the filter will be six different sounds, each of which represents six guitar strings. The design scheme uses a set of DSK TMS320C6713, Osiloscope, a Computer, and a Speaker.
